  1. This is your reference diagram. Your mission is to look through satellite imagery and indicate whether or not the satellite image looks like this reference image. Pay attention to the features in the diagram and see if you can find them in the satellite region. You can rotate it using the controls below.

  2. This is what one subregion will look like. Remember to look at not only the center, but also the edges and corners of these subregions for similar features.

  3. If you think the satellite imagery looks similar to the reference image, click the 'Yes / Maybe' button.

  4. If you do not think the satellite imagery looks similar to the reference image, click the 'No' button.

  5. After you make a decision, click the 'Next' button to view the next subregion.

  6. This map shows your progress throughout the 16 subregions. As you make your determinations, this map will update.

  7. Feel free to use any of the map controls available to you.

  8. After you have completed the task, this Generate Passcode button will become enabled. Please use this button to generate your unique passcode. Copy the passcode and paste it into Passcode field in the original HIT.
