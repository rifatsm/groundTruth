What is GroundTruth?

GroundTruth is a system that helps in geolocating an image with the help of crowd workers. Based on prior studies, we've found that crowd workers can reduce the search space (of satellite imagery) by up to 50% in 10 minutes.

How does it work?

Typically, you start off with a ground-level image or satellite image that you want to geolocate. You then draw an aerial diagram of the same and upload it into the system. Then, you'll need to designate an area on the map that you suspect contains the image. The size of this area depends on your budget. We provide this information to a large number crowd workers who decide whether a small portion of the map matches the aerial diagram and image. These decisions are aggregated and presented to you in the form of a colored grid which helps you quickly find the location of the image.
